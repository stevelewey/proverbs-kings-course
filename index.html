Common Questions

Straight Answers

How is this different from the 21-Day Proverbs Challenge?

The 21-Day Challenge is an on-ramp — it introduces you to the Proverbs framework and builds the foundational habits. The 8-Week Course is the full architecture. It delivers all eight leadership frameworks at depth, with integration tools, a personal profile, and a clear path forward. If you've completed the Challenge, you're ready for this.

Do I need to complete it in exactly eight weeks?

No. Self-guided means you set the pace. The course is structured for one framework per week, but your life and calendar lead. Some leaders move faster; some take longer with certain frameworks. The materials don't expire and you're not penalized for going deep.

Is this a religious program?

The frameworks are drawn from the book of Proverbs — one of the most practical leadership texts ever written. The course is practitioner-grounded and designed for marketplace leaders regardless of background. The language is direct and application-focused. No religious jargon. No assumptions about where you are spiritually.

What format does the course come in?

All materials are delivered as PDF documents — branded, clean, and designed for both screen and print. You'll receive instant access to the complete library upon enrollment.

What's the Peer Advisory Group mentioned in the What's Next doc?

It's one of six next-step options available after you complete the course. The Peer Advisory Group is a smaller, facilitated cohort for leaders who want to apply the frameworks in a peer accountability context. Details, schedule, and pricing are included in the What's Next Pathway document.

Is there a live or group version of this course?

Yes — the Proverbs & Kings 8-Week Cohort is the live, facilitated version with weekly sessions, group accountability, and direct access to Steve. The self-guided course is designed for leaders who want full flexibility. Both deliver the same eight frameworks; the experience and depth of engagement are different.

What if I have questions while going through the material?

Reach out directly at steve@lightbasedleadership.com. This is a personal brand built on relationship, not a faceless platform. If something's unclear or you want to talk through application, send a note.
